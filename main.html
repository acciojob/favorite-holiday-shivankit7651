Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, world-class art museums, and romantic ambiance. It attracts millions of visitors from around the world every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to masterpieces including the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Located atop Montmartre hill with amazing panoramas.
  5. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue known for shopping and vibrant atmosphere.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – Traditional French bread, crisp on the outside and soft inside.
  • Escargot – A unique French delicacy for adventurous eaters.
  • Crème Brûlée – A rich custard dessert topped with caramelized sugar.
  • Macarons – Colorful, sweet almond-based confections.
